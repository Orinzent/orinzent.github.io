Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

At Orinzent ("we", "us", or "our"), we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our app, FixLens.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the app.

  • Visual Data: Photos you upload for diagnosis.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your visual data strictly for the purpose of providing repair diagnosis and improving our AI models.

  • To generate troubleshooting steps.
  • To identify objects and parts.
  • To ensure app safety and security.

3. Data Storage & Deletion

Images uploaded for "Quick Scan" are processed temporarily and deleted from our servers after the session, unless you explicitly choose to save them to your history. You may request full data deletion at any time.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please email us at privacy@fixlens.com.

Account & Data Deletion

Control your data transparency.

According to Google Play and App Store requirements, users have the right to request the deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and data through the following methods:

Method 1: In-App

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will immediately deactivate your account and schedule your data for permanent deletion within 30 days.

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to support@fixlens.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include the email address associated with your account.

What Data is Deleted?

  • Your profile information (Name, Email).
  • Your saved scan history and chat logs.
  • Your subscription details.

Retention: We may retain certain data for legal reporting purposes for up to 90 days after the deletion request.
